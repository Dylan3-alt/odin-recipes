Mini Meatball & Pasta Marinara Soup

Description

This recipe is super flexible. Anelletti is the preferred pasta cut here for the ultimate nostalgia factor, but any small cut—like elbows or bowties—will do.

Ingredients

  • crushed red pepper flakes
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • sweet Italian sausage, casings discard, shaped into ½” balls
  • small dry pasta (such as anelletti)
  • packed baby spinach Freshly grated Parmesan, for serving

Steps

  1. In a large pot over medium heat, heat oil. Add onion, carrots, garlic, Italian seasoning, red pepper flakes, and season with salt and pepper. Cook, stirring occasionally, until vegetables soften, about 6 minutes.
  2. Add in marinara and chicken broth and bring to a simmer. Add sausage meatballs and pasta and stir until pasta is cooked al dente, stirring frequently, 9 to 12 minutes. Stir in spinach to wilt and remove from heat.
  3. Serve with freshly grated Parmesan.
