Chibwabwa

Description

Chibwabwa is one of the most local meals prepared in the central part of zambia, the meal originated from the pumpkin leaves, the leaves are then blended with the grinded groundnuts to make the meal delicious.

Ingredients

  • pumpkin leaves
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 4 c.grinded groundnuts
  • salt
  • onion

Steps

  1. in a medium sided pot put cleaned and chopped pumpkin leaves, alllow them to cook for 3-5 minutes
  2. lins out some water then add salt and tomatoes, onion and the groundnuts, then cover the pot the cook for another 5 minutes then stire to mix
  3. continue stiring in intervals of 3 minutes, the meal should be ready in 12 minutes time.
    4. BON APPETITE!
